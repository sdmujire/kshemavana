CONTEXT

Over the last decade, Yoga & Naturopathy has been growing by leaps and bounds with concept of wellness. Our desire to feel good, live longer, stress less and enjoy life has contributed to the proliferation of wellness catering to all taste and budgets. We are adopting an increasingly holistic approach to wellness or total health, taking into account, yoga, Naturopathy healing modalities, exercise, nutrition, relaxation, stress free and self-awareness.

Wellness as an important current & future driver for the Health industry and it is considered as 3 trillion dollar industry worldwide. Concept of Healing Centers will continue to be very important for the Wellness industry as well as for the new business opportunity hunters in India as well. Many consumers are fighting back, seeking better ways to take care of themselves, making lifestyle changes and taking a greater responsibility towards maintaining good health.

There are various changes that have been made by industry in response to wellness. Those who have introduced new services or products in response to this have reported a change in their marketing strategy. The tradition of Yoga & Naturopathy hospital as a place for healing, renewal, relaxation, and feeling well positions the Wellness industry as one of the most logical sectors to take advantage of ( and help lead ) the wellness movement. The core customer base for wellness almost directly overlaps with the primary customer market for Wellness.
